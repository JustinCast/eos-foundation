Value Reciepts

A non fungible token that enable recognition of good actors in the development of not only software but also the community as a whole. A badge of honor for your effort and work.

Membership

As a member you will be part, either by vote or acting as a custodian, of the decisions of the EOS Foundation. Feel free to reach out and be part of this new step for the EOS community.

What?

An autonomous, descentralized, non-profit organization that will enable the EOS community represent and promote itself for the good of many.

Why?

As seen on many other blockchains, a foundation is a vital part of the descentralized nature of this technology. We cannot leave many oportunities arise, move based on sheer luck and end up being wasted on opportunistic endevours. The community must act.

How?

The first step is to raise you hand and be proud of your work. Join the list of contributors to the EOS ecosystem or validate your known project. Be part of the initial group of Advisor and Genesis Steward Candidates who can help steer this project forward to a fully-launched DAO.
